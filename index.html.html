About Us

We are a Grade 10 STEM team working on a capstone project that focuses on developing a fire-resistant and sustainable building using Dendrocalamus stocksii bamboo.

Bamboo resists fire due to its internal structure composed of cellulose, hemicellulose, and lignin. These components promote charring instead of rapid combustion, while natural silica in bamboo provides an additional protective layer.
